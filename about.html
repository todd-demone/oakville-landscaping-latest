About Us

Oakville Landscaping was founded in 1997 by Kyle Caya. Today, Oakville Landscaping is one of the longest serving full service landscaping contractors in Oakville, and is a member of Landscape Ontario Horticultural Trades Association and the Canadian Nursery Landscape Association.

Oakville Landscaping's values include a strong commitment to quality work and superior customer service. It is a testament to these values that we have many loyal clients that have been with us since day one.

Testimonials

"Gorgeous Backyard Retreat..."

Two years ago, Kyle and his Oakville Landscaping team helped do a major overhaul of my back yard in the creation of my oasis.

The work was extensive as it involved adding pool copings, laying new interlock stones with a complicated pattern and incorporating an oddly shaped backyard. Kyle brought samples of stones and coping directly to the house so that we could see exactly how it would look with the lighting in the backyard. They were meticulous in each step of the way, from ensuring the base was solid to ensuring that each stone was cut to the proper specifics. This was of significant importance as the yard involved many curves and consequently many stones had to be cut specifically to fit the tiniest corners.

Each day they kept things tidy to prevent potential injury and always cleaned up at the end of the day. The team was also meticulous in ensuring proper fittings were found to accommodate what was being done in the yard. Adjustments had to be made related to the pool skimmers and they went out of their way to ensure that this was all done properly.

Because of weather, the backyard took a couple of weeks extra but the end result is great. I have a gorgeous backyard retreat that has been the envy of my neighbours.

-- Liz, Oakville
