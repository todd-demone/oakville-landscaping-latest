Landscape Design & Installation

Our landscape designer has the talent and expertise to design beautiful landscape designs that are sure to make a lasting impact for your home.

For each project, The Oakville Landscaping designer offers a unique design solution to meet our clients objectives, site conditions and budget constraints. We strive to provide creative and unique designs for each project.

Our landscaping services include (but are not limited to):

  • Tree planting
  • Annual & perennial planting
  • Sodding & seeding
  • Flower bed design
  • Armour stone
  • Trenching
  • Topsoil and fill
  • Irrigation systems
  • Outdoor lighting
  • Outdoor carpentry

For more information please call our office at 905-847-3504. Landscaping services such as patios, walkways and stonework are featured on our hardscaping services page.
