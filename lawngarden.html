Lawn & Garden Maintenance

Our lawn and garden maintenance team can expertly maintain and enhance your property. We can easily structure a maintenance program to fit your needs and budget.

Our lawn & garden maintenance services include (but are not limited to):

  • Lawn mowing and trimming
  • Leaf vacuuming
  • Tree and shrub removal
  • Flower bed edging
  • Tree, hedge & shrub trimming, pruning
  • Rototilling and cultivating
  • Mulch
  • Lawn aeration
  • De-thatching (lawn raking)
  • Weeding
  • Spring and fall cleanup
  • Disposal services
  • General labour

For more information please call our office at 905-847-3504.
